LODRUPIZZA - DISCOVER TASTE OF ITALY

We invite you to a little part if Italy in Gdansk, where we bake, hopefully, the tastiest pizza in our city!

Margherita

A classic Italian pizza with thin crust, tomato sauce, mozzarella, and fresh basil. A simple yet perfect balance of flavors!

Pepperoni

A spicy favorite topped with loads of pepperoni, gooey mozzarella, and rich tomato sauce. Perfect for those who love a little heat!

Hawaiian

A sweet and savory combo with juicy pineapple, ham, and melted cheese. A controversial choice, but loved by many!

Quattro Formaggi

A dream for cheese lovers, featuring a blend of mozzarella, gorgonzola, parmesan, and ricotta. Creamy, rich, and absolutely delicious!
